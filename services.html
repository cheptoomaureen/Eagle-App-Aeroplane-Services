Ordering from Your Seat

Ordering your meal or beverage is simple and can be done directly from your seat:

  • Access the in-flight entertainment system to view the menu.
  • Select your desired items and add them to your cart.
  • Proceed to checkout and choose your preferred payment method.
  • Your order will be prepared and served to you at your seat.
